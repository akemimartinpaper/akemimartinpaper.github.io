Her practice lives at the intersection of ancient craft and contemporary art — pulling fiber from water, pressing it into form, letting it dry into something that holds meaning before a single mark is made. She collaborates with other artists, makes and thinks out loud in her flagship basement studio, but also teaches papermaking at The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art, Boston University, Shepherd & Maudsleigh Studio and occasionally at The Penland School of Craft and Dieu Donné. 100% HANDMADE 0 RULES FOLLOWED ∞ FIBER SOURCES 1 OF A KIND TEACHING Workshops & Classes 01 Introduction to Papermaking Pull your first sheet from the vat.
